Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is the name of the oldest and best-known pasta shapes. It is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped,thicker than the tagliatelle.

Ingredients:

  • Ragù
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Pasta

Steps:

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottm of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  4. Spread an even layer of meat ragù.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
  6. Top it with a final layer of noodles,sauce and cheese.
